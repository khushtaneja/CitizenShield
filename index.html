How It Works

Report in Three Simple Steps

No complicated process — just three quick steps to make your community safer.

1. Observe

Notice something unusual? An unattended package, suspicious loitering, or any activity that seems out of place? Trust your instincts — it matters.

2. Report

Fill out our simple online form with the location, a brief description, and optionally attach an image. The entire process takes under two minutes.

3. Authorities Act

Your report is reviewed by authorized personnel who assess, verify, and take appropriate action. You contribute directly to national safety.

Why It Matters

Citizens Are the First Line of Defence

Law enforcement cannot be everywhere. Alert citizens noticing and reporting suspicious behaviour has historically prevented many incidents.

Community Awareness

Every citizen who reports helps build a network of awareness that makes communities harder to target.

Early Warning System

Timely reports enable authorities to respond before minor suspicious activities escalate into serious threats.

Secure & Confidential

All reports are handled with discretion. Your identity is protected and your information is stored securely.

System Features

What This Platform Offers

Mobile Friendly

Access the portal from any device — smartphone, tablet, or desktop. The design adapts to any screen size.

Instant Validation

Forms are validated immediately to ensure accurate and complete information reaches the authorities efficiently.

Admin Oversight

Authorities have a dedicated dashboard to track all reports, review details, and update statuses in real time.

Image Attachments

Citizens can attach photographs as evidence, making it easier for authorities to assess and respond appropriately.

Status Tracking

Each report has a clear status — Pending, Verified, or Resolved — so the admin always knows exactly what needs attention.

Firebase Cloud Database

Reports are stored in Firebase Firestore — a secure, scalable cloud database — ensuring data persists across all devices and sessions.
