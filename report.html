Tips for a Good Report

  • Be as specific as possible about the location
  • Describe the appearance and behaviour clearly
  • Mention the exact time of the incident if known
  • Attach an image if you have one — it helps greatly
  • Do not put yourself in danger to gather information
  • Report only factual observations, not assumptions
